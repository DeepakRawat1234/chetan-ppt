Industrial Cooling Applications
Enhance Productivity with Cost-Effective Factory Cooling Solutions

Factory managers and owners are increasingly focused on providing a comfortable working environment for employees during the manufacturing process. However, air conditioning an entire factory is often not a viable option due to the high initial costs and ongoing operational expenses. This is where Evaporative Air Cooling proves to be a cost-effective alternative.

During peak summer months, temperatures in industrial facilities can rise above 45°C, making it difficult for employees to work efficiently and safely. A well-designed Arctic Factory Cooling System can reduce temperatures to around 27°C in a highly economical manner, improving comfort levels and boosting productivity.

At Arctic Factory Cooling, our team of experts conducts a thorough study of your factory's layout to recommend the most optimal cooling solution. Based on your specific needs, we offer a range of tailored options, including:

•Ducted Cool Air Flow: Delivers uniform cooling to work areas.
•Portable Coolers: Provides on-spot cooling for targeted areas.
•Side/Top Discharge Air Coolers: Ideal for broader coverage or challenging factory layouts.
•Indirect/Direct Evaporative Cooling: For additional temperature reduction, particularly useful during monsoon or higher humidity months.
Our solutions not only help reduce temperatures but also work to improve air quality by minimizing suspended dust in the shop floor environment, ensuring both employee comfort and safety.

With a variety of products and cooling solutions available, our team will work closely with you to identify the best options tailored to your factory’s requirements. As Factory Cooling Experts, we are committed to providing you with cost-effective, efficient, and sustainable cooling solutions that will enhance both employee well-being and factory productivity

Commercial Air Cooler Applications:
Ensure Comfort and Productivity in Every Space

When common facilities such as schools, kitchens, wedding halls, small movie theaters, showrooms, religious places, offices, restaurants, service centers, and other commercial spaces become hot and stuffy, it leads to a range of problems. Discomfort, reduced concentration, and a decrease in work accuracy are just a few of the negative impacts. Moreover, a less productive work environment and increased health risks become significant concerns when the temperature and air quality are not properly managed.

Stagnant indoor air further exacerbates the situation by promoting discomfort and encouraging the spread of viruses and bacteria in enclosed spaces. Poor ventilation and compromised air quality not only make the environment unpleasant but can also harm the health of occupants, contributing to higher illness rates and increased absenteeism. To maintain a healthy, productive, and comfortable environment, good indoor air quality (IAQ) is essential in commercial spaces. At the same time, this must be achieved in an economical way to ensure the health and comfort of all stakeholders—whether it's owners, tenants, employees, students, or customers.

Introducing cooled fresh air through a well-designed ducting arrangement in commercial spaces offers a cost-effective and efficient solution. This method ensures that the air quality is improved and that the space remains cool and comfortable, contributing to occupant delight. A planned air-cooling system helps prevent discomfort and boosts productivity by reducing the likelihood of illness and improving overall air quality.
